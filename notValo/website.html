About Valorant

VALORANT is an online FPS shooter video game developed by Riot. The premise of the game is to either set off a bomb or stop the bomb from exploding. Two teams of five compete together on one map with two to three 'sites' where the bomb can be 'planted.' One team is defending these sites, the other attacking. Each team has the same character options, though someperform better on either attacking or defending sides. Each game is played until one team has thirteen wins, which can take anywhere from thirty minutes to an hour. Before each round is a buy phase where players can purchase guns, shields/armor, and abilities. The amount of money a player has is determined by thier previous round's money and rewards for how well they did last round. Doing well for multiple rounds in a row results in a lot more money given, though there is a money reward for losing streaks to help teams get out of ruts.
The game was first announced under the codename Project A in October 2019, and was officially released on June 2, 2020.

Agents

Valorant features a diverse cast of characters known as Agents, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles.

Weapons

There are a variety of weapons available in Valorant, each with their own strengths and weaknesses.

Game Modes

Valorant currently has several game modes, including the standard Competitive mode, as well as the casual Unrated and Spike Rush modes.

Agent Types
Duelists - Duelists are expected to make first contact with the enemy team. Their abilities include some way to take space, usually with their bodies through movement, or another ability to get kills and win duels.

Initiators - The goal of the Initiators is to support their duelists as much as they can. This can include throwing flash grenades, revealing the position of enemies, and stunning the opposition for easy kills.

Controllers - Controllers main purpose is to dice up the battlefield using a variety of smoke grenades and mollies, or molatovs, to drive opponents from key positons. Blocking enemy sight can be crutial in taking control of area quickly and effectively.

Sentinals - Protecting the rear of the parties advance are the Sentinals. Their abilities include ebverything from cameras, machines to alert if someone is flanking, or barricades to stop their advance.
