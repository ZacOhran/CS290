Agent List

Duelists

- Duelists are expected to entry, meaning they are the first ones to enter the sight.
Most have some sort of ability to get away from the inevitable bullets fired at them.
Some have movement abilities, while others have flashes or nearisght to limit the
vision of the enemies.
- Jett, Reyna, Phoenix, Yoru, Raze, Neon

Controllers

- Controllers main goal is to break line of sight from the enemy team to theirs. Their
abilities consist of what are called 'smokes,' coming from smoke grenades common
in video games. Many have one use of their abilities, so Controllers must have the
game sense and timing down to be effective.
- Viper, Brimstone, Omen, Harbor, Astra
Sentinals
- Sentinals are players who watch behind the party, usually through an ability that
hidners the enemy team from coming from behind, or at least slow them down.
Chamber, Killjoy, Cypher, Sage
Initiators
- Initiators are in charge of helping the duelist get the team onto the sight. They
usually have abilities similar to flash grenades that can give the team some room to
move around while the enemies can't see. Others have abilities to reveal the location
of enemies as they hide behind cover, sometimes highlighting them from behind cover.
- Kay/o, Skye, Fade, Breach, Sova
